# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 7, 2026

## What Data We Collect

**None.** This app:
- ❌ Does NOT collect any personal data
- ❌ Does NOT send data to any servers
- ❌ Does NOT use analytics or tracking
- ❌ Does NOT require an account

## Where Your Data Lives

All your class schedules are stored **locally** on your computer:
- **macOS:** `~/Library/Application Support/class-reminder-app/classes.json`
- Your data never leaves your device unless you export it

## What Permissions We Use

| Permission | Why |
|------------|-----|
| Notifications | Send class reminders |
| File System | Save/load your schedule locally |

## Third-Party Services

This app uses **Electron** (open-source framework). No third-party services are contacted.

## AI Features (Class Companion Overlay)

If you choose to use the AI Q&A feature:
- You may add your own API key (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.)
- Questions are sent directly to the API provider
- We do NOT store or see your questions
- Review the API provider's privacy policy

## Children's Privacy

This app is safe for students of all ages. No data is collected from anyone.

## Changes to This Policy

We'll update this file if anything changes. For now: **no data collection, ever.**

## Contact

This is a student project. If you have questions, ask the person who shared it with you!

**TL;DR:** Your data stays on your computer. We don't collect anything.
